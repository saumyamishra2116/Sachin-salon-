Our Story

Founded in 2015, Glamour Lounge began as a small boutique salon with a big vision - to redefine beauty standards in Nairobi. What started as a single-chair studio has blossomed into Nairobi's premier destination for luxury hair care and styling.

Our founder, Grace Mwangi, envisioned a space where clients could escape the everyday and be transformed. Today, our team of award-winning stylists continues this tradition, combining the latest techniques with time-honored traditions to create looks that are as unique as you are.

We believe beauty should be accessible, enjoyable, and most importantly, personalized. Every service we offer is tailored to enhance your natural beauty and reflect your individual style.
